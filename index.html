VAR™

The video assistant referee is a referee that watches the match through special cameras. The var helps with identifying goal validity, penalty kicks, red cards and offsides. Because the referee on the field cant always see whats happening the VAR helps to make these important decisions. The VAR does not step in when a player is given a yellow card, a corner is given or a faul has been committed. The field referee does not always have to take the advice of the VAR. More info.

VR in sport

Virtual Reality is an important technology in sport. VR is used in alot of sports, from formula 1 racing to rugby. VR helps players practice where ever they are. For example, VR is used by formula drivers to explore the track without actually having to drive on it. This is important because a driver only gets a limited time for exploring the track on track. VR gives drivers more practice time for difficult corners. In rugby VR is used to practice line-outs, high balls and first phase attacks. all without needing an actual field. More info.
